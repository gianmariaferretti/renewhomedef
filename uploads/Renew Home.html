Your questions, answered

No catch. Just comfort.

Can I turn it off if I'm cold?
Always. The whole system is comfort-first — temperature shifts stay within about 1°F of your setpoint, and you can override any event with a single tap in your app. You stay in control, every time.
Are you taking remote control of my home?
Only with your explicit permission, and only for tiny, comfort-safe adjustments during grid-stress moments. It's transparent and permission-based — you can opt out at any time, no questions asked.
Do I have to track time-of-use tariffs or schedules?
Never. Renew Home is fully automatic — no scheduling, no learning curve, no figuring out when energy is cheapest. Our AI handles the timing so you don't have to think about it.
Does it actually lower my bill?
Yes — households save roughly $100 a year on average, with zero effort. A real-time dashboard shows exactly how much you've saved and how much CO₂ you've avoided, so the impact is always visible.
Is there any cost or hardware to buy?
None. It's free forever and works with the smart thermostat you already own. No device to purchase, no installation, no subscription.

Procurement questions

Built for grid reliability teams.

How accurate is the balancing?
We achieve 95%+ dispatch accuracy through predictive AI orchestration — load forecasting plus real-time control across millions of devices — so you get contractually-guaranteed MW you can bid into capacity markets with confidence.
How does this compare to building a peaker plant?
Building peaker capacity costs $1M+/MW and takes years of permitting. Renew Home delivers equivalent dispatchable capacity at roughly 50% of utility-scale battery cost, online in months instead of years — no construction, no permitting risk.
What about low enrollment and high customer-acquisition cost?
You plug directly into an existing base of 5M+ pre-enrolled, digitally-engaged homes. Our bundled "Capacity + Customer Acquisition" offer also gives deregulated markets a low-cost channel to acquire residential customers.
Who owns compliance, M&V, and operations?
We do. Renew Home owns the technology, end-user operations, M&V reporting, and regulatory filings — a full risk transfer. You integrate via our DERMS and real-time dispatch APIs and receive market-ready capacity.
Can you bid into ISO/RTO capacity markets?
Yes. Aggregated load is delivered as predictable, contractually-guaranteed MW suitable for wholesale capacity auctions, demand-response events, and ancillary services across deregulated and regulated markets.
